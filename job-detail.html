Senior Product Designer

TechCorp Inc.

Remote Full-time Design
Apply on App

Job Description

We're looking for an experienced Senior Product Designer to join our innovative team. You'll work on cutting-edge products that impact millions of users worldwide.

What You'll Do

  • Design user experiences for web and mobile applications
  • Collaborate with product managers, engineers, and stakeholders
  • Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs
  • Conduct user research and usability testing
  • Maintain and evolve our design system
  • Mentor junior designers and contribute to design culture

Requirements

  • 5+ years of experience in product design
  • Strong portfolio showcasing UX/UI design work
  • Proficiency in Figma, Sketch, or similar tools
  • Experience with user research and testing
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills
  • Bachelor's degree in Design or related field

Benefits

  • Competitive salary: $80,000 - $120,000
  • Health, dental, and vision insurance
  • 401(k) with company match
  • Flexible work hours and remote work options
  • Professional development budget
  • Unlimited PTO

About TechCorp Inc.

TechCorp is a leading technology company focused on innovation and user experience. We've been building products that matter for over 10 years, and we're looking for talented individuals to join our mission.
